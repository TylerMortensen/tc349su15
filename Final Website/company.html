About IHS

Mission, Vision & Values

Leading by example, focused on patient centered care, Innovative Healthcare Solutions provides quality leadership to healthcare executives. To improve operational performance by delivering measurable, sustainable results. Improvements today create success tomorrow.

Leadership Team

Innovative Healthcare Solutions leadership team is made up of individuals who are passionate about improving performance in the healthcare industry. We create integrated solutions with healthcare leadership to drive change and sustain positive outcomes for their organization.

Company History

Created in 2012 Innovative Healthcare Solutions (IHS) has been committed to organization success. IHS began as a consulting firm assisting hospital executives to improve performance in patient throughput and flow, clinical and quality excellence. It continues to serve organizations with this effective outcomes driven approach as it mentors nursing leaders to sustain the changes needed in the hospital environment.
